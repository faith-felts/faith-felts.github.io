Healthy Hearts

Healthy Hearts brings free, high-quality healthcare services to low-income and uninsured communities in the Austin area through mobile clinics and public wellness events.

With the help of dedicated medical professionals and volunteers, Healthy Hearts offers screenings, vaccinations, and mental health support where it's needed most. Join us to ensure no one is left behind when it comes to healthcare.
